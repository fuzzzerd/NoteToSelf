*Someone [asked on Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com/q/1236049):*

> [http://www.s3fm.com/](http://www.s3fm.com/) is really useful for uploading/viewing files for personal viewing.
> 
> I was wondering if there was something good for social networks (using Amazon s3 or something similar), where it will only show the subset of files uploaded by a specific user (for example, limit access to the user's specific bucket).
> 
> Can s3fm be adapted to this solution? or is there something else out there?

*I posted the following answer:*

I believe you would need to build your own system to do this. What you use doesn't really matter, you could use S3, Azure, etc as your base storage "cloud."
