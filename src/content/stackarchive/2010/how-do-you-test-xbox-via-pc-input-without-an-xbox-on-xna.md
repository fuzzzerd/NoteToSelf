*I [asked this on Game Development](https://gamedev.stackexchange.com/q/3922):*

Is it safe to just hookup a wired Xbox controller to my PC via USB? Do I need to buy a special PC Xbox controller? I've read conflicting reports. Any help is appreciated.

---

> [wkerslake answered](https://gamedev.stackexchange.com/a/3924) (8 upvotes):
>
> You can use an Xbox wired controller, or you can pick up the wireless receiver which plugs into your USB port and lets you use the wireless controllers with windows.
> 
> Here is a [link](http://www.google.com/products/catalog?q=Xbox+360+Wireless+Gaming+Receiver+for+Windows&cid=9984239196350938768&ei=2tyTTNTMEIm4iwTKuqGdCw&sa=title&ved=0CAcQ8wIwADgA#p) to the wireless receiver.
